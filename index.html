About Us

West Norwood Mosque is a place of worship, peace, and unity in the heart of West Norwood. Our mission is to serve the local community, offering religious guidance, support, and a welcoming environment for everyone. The mosque provides a variety of services and activities, including Islamic education, charitable outreach, and spiritual development programs.
